House & Appliances

A few small things to keep the place happy for you and the next guests:

  • No smoking indoors — the balcony is all yours, please use the ashtray.
  • Rinse the sand off at the ground-floor beach shower before coming up. Quartz sand gets everywhere!
  • Quiet hours after 10 PM — sound carries between the units and over the water.
  • Trash goes down the chute at the end of the hall; recycling bins are in the garage.

Appliance quick-notes:

  • Thermostat is by the kitchen — please keep it at 72°F or higher when the A/C runs so it doesn't freeze up in the humidity.
  • Beach gear (chairs, umbrella, cooler, boogie boards) is in the front closet.
  • Washer/dryer is in the hall closet; pods are in the basket on the shelf.

Beach Service

Beach service is included March through October — two loungers and an umbrella set up for you daily, so there's no dragging gear across the hot sand.

  • Where: Look for the attendant's chairs on the sand directly in front of the building.
  • What's included: Two chairs and one umbrella, set out each morning by 9 AM.
  • Just give the attendant the unit name — the set is reserved under Pure DESTINation.
🌊
Beach flags matter here: green is calm, yellow is caution, red means dangerous surf, and double red means the water is closed. Destin flies them daily near the beach access — always check before you swim.

Food & Local Favorites

Welcome to the World's Luckiest Fishing Village. The Destin Harbor and HarborWalk Village are the heart of the action — here are a few local favorites to point you in the right direction:

Eat

Dewey Destin's Seafood

Family-run since the 1940s by the family the town is named for. A no-frills waterside shack with some of the freshest fish around. Cash is king — bring some.

Eat

The Back Porch

A Destin classic since 1974 and the birthplace of the amberjack sandwich. Casual, right on the Gulf, unbeatable water views.

Eat

Boshamps Seafood & Oyster House

Southern-style Gulf-to-table on the harbor with a big outdoor deck and live music on weekends. Great for groups.

Sip

The Edge SkyBar

Rooftop bar over the harbor — arrive about 30 minutes before sunset for a seat. Live music most nights.

Sip

AJ's Seafood & Oyster Bar

Lively harborfront hangout — steam pots, Boom Boom Shrimp, and nightly live music. The go-to for a fun night out.

Do

Crab Island

The famous shallow sandbar just off the Destin bridge — rent a pontoon or catch a tour and float in the emerald shallows.

Do

Henderson Beach State Park

Protected sugar-white dunes and nature trails right in town — a quieter beach morning away from the crowds.

Do

Deep-sea fishing charter

It's the World's Luckiest Fishing Village for a reason. Half- and full-day charters leave from the harbor daily — book ahead in season.

🍩
Sweet tip: Start a morning at the Donut Hole Bakery Café — a longtime Destin institution. Go early; the line moves but it builds.
