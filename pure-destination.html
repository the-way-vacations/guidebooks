Finding the South entrance: Southbay has two entrances off Hwy 98 that share this address — you want the South entrance, where Unit 80 is, and it's easy to miss from the highway. Your turn is Sandprint Drive, directly across Hwy 98 from the Raceway gas station.

Heading east (from Fort Walton Beach / Okaloosa Island): stay in the right lane. Pass Southbay's North entrance — the first entrance off Hwy 98 — and continue to the Sunsations plaza; Sandprint Drive is your right turn just past it. (The Raceway sits directly across the highway from the turn, but the median can hide it eastbound — go by Sunsations.)

Heading west (from Miramar Beach / Sandestin): stay in the left lane. When the Raceway comes up on your right, turn left onto Sandprint Drive, directly across from it. If you pass the Raceway and see the 98 Palms plaza on your right, you've just missed the turn — loop back.

Then: follow Sandprint as it curves around the lake and dead-ends into the Southbay South entrance — ignore the other gated turnoffs along the way, which lead to other properties. At the entrance, a parking arm (not a gate) raises with the code from your check-in instructions.
🅿️
Parking: Parking is in the complex's South lot, which needs a gate code for entry (included in your check-in instructions). Your unit has one reserved spot marked 80, plus one unmarked guest spot — any guest spot in the lot works. Parking passes are provided in the unit; keep one displayed on your dashboard at all times.

House & Appliances

A few small things to keep the place happy for you and the next guests:

  • No smoking or vaping inside the unit or on the patio.
  • Quiet hours are 10 PM to 7 AM — set by the City of Destin and strictly enforced.
  • Pool hours are 10 AM to 10 PM, and the pool closes promptly at 10.
  • No pets in the pool, on the tennis/pickleball courts, or on the beach.
  • Trash: bag and tie everything (including pizza boxes, cartons, and store bags) and set it at the end of the unit sidewalk by 10 AM for daily pickup — never after 10 AM and never overnight. After 10 AM, take bags to the dumpster by the pickleball courts (right out the front door).

Around the townhome:

  • Thermostat is a Honeywell touchscreen in the corner near the sectional couch. Use the up/down arrows to change the temperature, or tap Mode to switch between heat, cool, and off, then Done.
  • Beach gear: check the unlocked shed at the back of the patio for any beach extras. What's there changes from stay to stay — you may find a few beach chairs or odds and ends, but it's not guaranteed, so bring your own must-haves.
  • Grill: the patio has a propane grill. Uncover it before lighting, and let it cool completely before re-covering. BBQ tools are in the shed, along with a spare propane tank in case the one on the grill runs empty mid-cookout.
  • Patio string lights are controlled by the switch just inside, next to the patio door — please don't unplug them from the outside receptacle.
  • Washer/dryer is the full-size unit in the main-floor closet by the front door; two starter pods are provided.
🏝️
Pool & courts: The pool is directly behind the building, across from Unit 80. Coming straight out the front door, enter from the right (south) side — no code needed; the left (north) gate needs the pool code from your check-in instructions. The tennis/pickleball court is straight ahead to the right out of the unit (court code also in your instructions). The pool is heated seasonally at the HOA's discretion — typically Thanksgiving week and from late December through New Year's, then off until it resumes around February–March. The HOA may turn the heat off at any time.

Beach Service

Free beach service is included with your stay March through October — two chairs and one umbrella set out for you daily, so there's no dragging gear across the hot sand.

  • Where: A chair attendant is stationed on the sand. Just let them know you're staying in Unit 80.
  • If the attendant isn't there: Feel free to take any empty set of chairs that doesn't have a tag on the back or towels laid across it — the attendant will come by to ask which unit you're in.
  • What's included: Two chairs and one umbrella, set out each morning (seasonal, March–October).
  • Beach access: About a 2-minute walk. Exit the unit, take a right, and head diagonally toward the left corner of the complex to reach the beach walkover.
⛱️
Need more chairs? Your stay comes with one free setup (two chairs and one umbrella). Extra setups run $45/day for two chairs and an umbrella, with a price break for multiple days. Two ways to book through Sunset Beach Service:

1 · Online: reserve at sunsetbeachservice.com ↗ and be sure to select “Southbay” under “where are you staying.” The site has no field for a unit number, so message us once you've booked and we'll let them know it's for Unit 80.

2 · By text: text Bill Monson at (850) 585-1577 with your dates and the number of chairs, and mention you're staying at Southbay, Unit 80. They'll be set up and ready — pay on the beach.

The beach belongs to Southbay's owners and guests, and everyone using it follows the same HOA and county rules:

  • No tents, and don't set personal chairs or umbrellas in front of the beach-service set (HOA rule).
  • No glass or breakable containers, and no pets (county ordinance).
  • No alcohol for anyone under 21 — the Sheriff's Department monitors this.
  • Fill in any holes you dig, and take everything with you at the end of the day; anything left is removed and disposed of (county ordinance).
🌊
Beach flags matter here: green is calm, yellow is caution, red means dangerous surf, double red means the water is closed, and purple warns of dangerous marine life. Flags fly daily near the beach access — always check before you swim. Want daily flag updates by text? Text BEACH to 44144.
